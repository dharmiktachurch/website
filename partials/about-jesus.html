JESUS

The Way, The Truth, and The Life.

Who is Jesus?

Jesus Christ, the Son of God, came to Earth to save us. He healed the sick, loved the broken, taught truth, and gave His life to reconnect us with God. Through His resurrection, He defeated death and brought us hope that lasts forever.

Love

Jesus loves you unconditionally. His love is not based on what you've done, but who you are.

Forgiveness

No matter your past, Jesus offers forgiveness and a fresh start with a clean heart.

Purpose

In Jesus, your life has meaning, direction, and eternal value that goes beyond this world.

What is Salvation?

Salvation is a free gift from God. By believing in Jesus, repenting from your sins, and choosing to follow Him, you receive forgiveness, new life, and eternal hope.

“If you declare with your mouth, ‘Jesus is Lord,’ and believe in your heart that God raised him from the dead, you will be saved.” — Romans 10:9 (NIV)

A Simple Prayer

Dear Jesus,

I believe You are the Son of God. Thank You for loving me and dying for me.

Please forgive me and come into my heart. Be my Savior and Lord. I want to follow You forever.

Amen.

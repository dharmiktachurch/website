Our Mission and Vision

We are committed to spreading the love of Christ through our worship, our community outreach, and our personal relationships. Our core values include love, service, and unity in Christ.

  • Love: We love God and our neighbors, embracing all people in the name of Jesus.
  • Service: We strive to serve our community and meet their spiritual and physical needs.
  • Unity: We believe in the power of the body of Christ coming together in harmony.

Our History

Dharmikta Mandali was founded with a vision to reach every heart with the gospel of Jesus Christ. Over the years, we have grown from a small group of believers to a vibrant, loving community committed to service and outreach.

In the early years, our church focused on providing spiritual guidance and support to those in need. Today, we continue to expand our ministries, from youth groups to outreach programs, ensuring that we impact every corner of our community.

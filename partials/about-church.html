About Dharmikta Mandali

Founded in 2009 AD

Our Church

Dharmikta Mandali is a Christ-centered church rooted in worship, discipleship, and service. Since 2009 AD, we’ve been nurturing a community of believers through teaching, outreach, and love.

We believe in the unity of believers and the power of prayer to transform lives and communities.

Our Mission and Vision

We are committed to spreading the love of Christ through our worship, our community outreach, and our personal relationships. Our core values include love, service, and unity in Christ.

  • Love: We love God and our neighbors, embracing all people in the name of Jesus.
  • Service: We strive to serve our community and meet their spiritual and physical needs.
  • Unity: We believe in the power of the body of Christ coming together in harmony.

Our History

Dharmikta Mandali was founded in 2009 with 25 believers who gathered in a small rented room in Bhanjyang, Lalitpur. Through continuous prayer, God revealed Proverbs 14:34, which became the foundation for our name – Dharmikta Mandali (Righteousness Church).

Within a month, the room was full, so we rented an additional room and joined them. Just two months later, we had to expand again as more people joined the fellowship.

As the church continued to grow, we moved to Bhaisepati, where we:

  • First moved our church on rented Space
  • Later relocated to another rented land and built a second church building
  • Finally moved to our current permanent space built on our own land

Today, we gather freely to worship and serve, continuing the journey that began with prayer and faith. We are thankful for God's guidance and the growth He has brought to Dharmikta Mandali.

About Dharmikta Mandali

Founded in 1990, our mission is to spread God's love across our community.

Who We Are

Dharmikta Mandali is a Christ-centered church serving the local community with love, faith, and purpose. We are a diverse congregation united by our belief in God's grace and the power of fellowship. Our mission is to share the Gospel, strengthen believers, and impact lives through worship, prayer, and outreach.

Our Mission

To make disciples, build leaders, and serve the world through God's truth and love.

Our Vision

A community transformed by the Gospel, living in faith and serving in love.
